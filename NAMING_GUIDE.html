ðŸ’¡ Important Tips

  • Use underscores (_) to separate fields, not spaces or dashes
  • File extension must be .jpg, .jpeg, or .png
  • Use uppercase for city codes and camera positions
  • Dates must be in YYYYMMDD format (ISO 8601)
  • No special characters except underscore (_)
  • Be consistent with naming across all images
  • Double-check spelling before uploading
  • The system will flag non-compliant names but still accept them
